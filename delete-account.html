Super Pool — Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 16, 2026 · Applies to the Super Pool game by Easy Technology

This page explains how to delete your Super Pool account and the data associated with it. It applies to the Super Pool game (package com.easytech.superpool) published by Easy Technology.

1. Delete your account from inside the app

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open Super Pool and sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Go to Settings → Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm at the two-step confirmation prompt.

Your account is deleted immediately and the action cannot be reversed.

2. Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, you can request deletion by email. Send a message to support@easytechcompany.com with the subject "Delete my Super Pool account" and include the email address or player ID linked to your account so we can locate it. We will verify ownership and complete the deletion within 30 days.

Guest players: if you never created an email account, your data is stored only on your device under an anonymous guest ID. Uninstalling the app, or using Settings → Profile → Delete Account, removes that local data.

3. What data is deleted

  • Account data — email address and password hash.
  • Profile data — username, avatar, level, and XP.
  • Gameplay data — match history, currency balances, owned cues and cosmetics, and daily-bonus state.
  • Diagnostic identifiers linked to your account.

Voice chat audio is streamed in real time and is never recorded or stored, so there is nothing to delete.

4. What may be retained, and for how long

After deletion, residual backup copies may persist for up to 30 days before being permanently purged. We may retain a minimal record where required by law (for example, purchase/transaction records held by Apple App Store or Google Play, which we do not control).
