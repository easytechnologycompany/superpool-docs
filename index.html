Frequently asked questions

  • How do I play? Tap "Play 1 on 1" or "Practice Offline", drag on the table to aim, then release to shoot. Pot all your balls and finish with the 8 to win.
  • Do I need an account? No — you can play instantly as a guest. Signing in with email lets you save progress across devices.
  • I had a problem with a purchase. Coins and cash are virtual items used inside the game. If a purchase didn't credit, email us with your receipt and we'll help.
  • How do I delete my account? Go to Settings → Profile → Delete Account, or see the account deletion page.
